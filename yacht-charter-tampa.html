Why Charter from Tampa

Tampa's Gateway to Open Water

Tampa is one of Florida's most vibrant cities — and its waterfront is world-class. Our marina in St. Petersburg is just a quick 20-minute drive from downtown Tampa across the Howard Frankland Bridge, giving you instant access to a luxury yacht charter without the hassle of Miami traffic or Keys logistics.

Once aboard, you'll cruise the stunning waters of Tampa Bay with views of the Tampa skyline, Bayshore Boulevard, Davis Islands, and beyond. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, hosting a bachelorette party, or treating your team to an unforgettable corporate event — our 52ft Marquis Flybridge is the finest private charter boat Tampa Bay has to offer.
