The Sunset Experience

Florida's Best Sunsets, From the Water

The Gulf Coast of Florida is famous for producing the most spectacular sunsets in the state — and there is no better way to experience golden hour than aboard a private yacht. As the sun dips toward the horizon, the sky transforms into a canvas of warm golds, deep oranges, and vivid pinks reflected across the calm waters of Tampa Bay.

Our 52ft Marquis Flybridge gives you three expansive viewing decks to take it all in. Settle into the flybridge for an unobstructed 360-degree panorama, relax on the aft deck with a drink in hand, or watch from the comfort of the climate-controlled salon through floor-to-ceiling windows. With a premium high-quality stereo setting the mood and up to 13 guests sharing the moment, this is sunset the way it was meant to be experienced.

Gulf Coast Golden Hour

Florida's west coast faces the sunset directly — no buildings, no trees, just open water and sky. The Gulf produces sunsets rated among the best in the entire country.
